Precision Auto Care and Body Repair

Located in Brisbane, Precision Auto Care and Body Repair is you premier automotive mechanic shop in the San Francisco Bay Area. We specalize in fleet vehicle maintance, BMW tuning, performance modifications, and more.

The Quote Process

From start to finish, we work with you every step of the way

Vehicle diagnosis

First, we need to take a look at your vehicle to start the diagnosis process and do a point-to-point vehicle inspection.

Cost estimate

After the diagnosis, our team will provide you with an accurate part list and quote on the estimated cost to repair the vehicle. We have competitive pricing on labor and parts that you won't find anywhere else.

Accept the work order and pickup your car

We are committed to guaranteeing your complete satisfaction with our services, aiming not only to exceed your expectations but also earn your trust to refer us to friends and family. Furthermore, we provide a parts warranty for select items, reinforcing our commitment to the quality of our work.

BMW Tuning and Maintenance

With years of expertise in modifying, tuning, and wiring BMWs, our seasoned team can help make your dreams a reality. Whether you require fundamental BMW repair and maintenance services or have a desire to enhance performance with mods like a Reflex controller, port injection, or MHD flash tuning.

Fleet Vehicle Maintenance

Elevate the longevity and performance of your Lincoln fleet with our comprehensive maintenance services, tailored to meet the unique demands of your premium vehicles, ensuring smooth operations and a reliable fleet presence.

Experts at American Performance

Precision Auto Care stands out as a leader in American car performance, demonstrating unparalleled expertise and a proven track record in delivering exceptional services to enhance the power, efficiency, and overall performance of your cherished American vehicles.

Foreign Performance
